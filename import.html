Microchip
All pets being imported to or exported from India must be Microchipped. India uses the standard ISO 11784/ISO 11785 microchip.
Immunizations
Basic and Mandatory Vaccinations
Before entering India, your pet must be vaccinated against certain diseases. This is done to prevent the transmission of potentially infectious zoonotic diseases from animal to animal/animal to person. Remember to get your pet vaccinated according to the timeline of your journey, as some vaccinations may require to be tested for (Ex: some countries have a cool off period of 30 days at animal quarantine, which can affect your itinerary).
  • Vaccinations for Dogs
  • Vaccinations for Cats
  • Rabies, a valid vaccination applied according to instruction of vaccine manufacturer, initial dose should not be applied before 12 weeks of animal age.
  • Canine Distemper Virus (CDV)
  • Canine Parvo Virus
  • Infectious Canine Hepatitis
  • Leptospirosis (Can be dispensed with and replaced by lab test in case that the exporting country does not apply vaccination)
