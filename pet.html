Pet Relocation in India: Your Complete Guide to a Safe and Stress-Free Journey

Moving Your Pet with Moveroo – Because They're Family Too

Relocating your home is a big change—but moving with your beloved pet? That's a responsibility we understand deeply. Whether it's a dog, cat, rabbit, or another furry (or feathered) friend, Moveroo ensures that your pet's journey is safe, comfortable, and fully compliant with all legal and health regulations.

Why Pet Relocation Needs Extra Care

Transporting your pet isn't just about booking a ticket and packing a crate. From obtaining the right permissions to ensuring your pet is healthy for the journey, the relocation process involves important legal and medical preparations—especially when moving across Indian states or to another country.

That's where we step in, making the process hassle-free for pet parents by managing the paperwork, logistics, and emotional comfort of your pet.

What Documents Do You Need for Pet Relocation in India?

To legally and safely relocate your pet, you'll need to arrange the following:

  • Updated vaccination certificates (Rabies, DHPPi, FVRCP, etc.)
  • A health fitness certificate from a certified veterinarian
  • Microchip registration (mandatory for most international travel)
  • Pet Travel NOC (No Objection Certificate) from the Animal Quarantine and Certification Service (AQCS)
  • Import/export permits (for international relocation)

Moveroo assists you through each step, ensuring you have both digital and printed copies of all necessary documents ready for inspections.

Understanding the Pet Travel NOC in India

The Pet Travel NOC is one of the most essential documents for pet relocation. Issued by AQCS, this certificate confirms your pet is fit to travel and has cleared all health checks.

At Moveroo, we can handle the NOC application process on your behalf, saving you from the hassle of multiple appointments and follow-ups.

Pet-Friendly Airlines in India: What Are Your Options?

Different airlines have different pet policies. Some of the popular pet-friendly airlines in India include:

  • Air India – Allows pets in-cabin (for small pets) and cargo.
  • Vistara – Permits small pets in-cabin on select routes.
  • SpiceJet – Offers pet relocation in cargo.

We'll help you choose the right airline, ensuring your pet's comfort, safety, and compliance with weight and crate size regulations.

Preparing Your Pet: Health Checks and Vaccines

Before your pet travels, they must undergo a complete health checkup. Key requirements include:

  • Rabies vaccine (at least 30 days before departure)
  • Core vaccinations
  • Deworming (within 7 days of travel)
  • A "fit-to-fly" certificate
  • Microchipping for international travel

Our team helps coordinate these vet visits and ensures your pet meets the travel health standards.

Breed-Specific Travel Restrictions to Keep in Mind

Certain breeds, especially brachycephalic (snub-nosed) dogs like Pugs, Bulldogs, and Shih Tzus, may face travel restrictions due to breathing concerns. Some countries and airlines also limit transport of specific dog breeds considered aggressive or high-risk.

Moveroo will help you navigate these breed-specific travel rules and find the safest travel options.

Will My Pet Be Quarantined?

If you're moving your pet internationally, some countries and airports require an animal quarantine process during entry or exit. Our team ensures your documentation is in order to avoid unnecessary delays and reduce the chances of extended quarantine stays.

Keeping Your Pet Emotionally Comfortable During Relocation

Beyond logistics, your pet's emotional well-being matters too. Here's how we help prepare your pet for the move:

  • Recommend gradual crate training before the journey
  • Suggest practice rides to mimic travel
  • Encourage keeping familiar toys and blankets during transit
  • Advise sticking to regular feeding and bathroom schedules

The goal? A calm, secure, and happy pet during travel.

Domestic vs. International Pet Relocation

Relocating your pet within India is usually simpler, while international moves require more paperwork and coordination. In both cases, we handle:

  • Airline-compliant travel crates
  • Veterinary checks and certificates
  • Travel NOCs
  • Coordination with pet-friendly airlines

For international relocation, we also manage customs documentation, destination country import rules, and post-arrival requirements.
