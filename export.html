AQCS MANDATES
Things to Remember Before Visiting
All the necessary documents must be submitted self-attested/ by authorized person (Original and Xerox copy) to the Quarantine office with Pet animal 7 days prior to embarkation for obtaining the NOC with prior appointment.
If the pet is not of eligible age for any vaccinations, the vaccination record of mother must be produced along with pet.

The application Performa must be filled in separately for each pet with all relevant details.
AQCS Requirements for Export Pet
  • The pet for examination by the officer.
  • Veterinary health certificate (Fit to fly by road/Air/Train) from any Local Registered Veterinarian.
  • Microchip Certificate.
  • Last one year vaccination details of the pet.
  • Passport copies of the owner.
  • Air ticket copy of the owner.
  • Airway bill in case the pet is going through cargo.
  • If owner is not available to visit AQCS then the authorized person shall submit an authority letter.
  • Any specific requirements of importing country (like- Import permit, Blood titer test etc.).
  • 2 Post Card size Photographs (Light facing) size 4x6 inches of the pet.
  • A photocopy of all the documents along with original has to be brought to AQCS.
  • Export certificate is valid for 10 days from the date of issue / Examination in AQCS.
