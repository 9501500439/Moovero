We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and secure online payment gateways. A detailed cost breakdown is provided before you confirm your booking. For international relocations, a deposit may be required to secure your pet’s travel arrangements.

What documents are needed for international pet relocation?
You will typically need your pet’s vaccination records, a valid health certificate from a licensed vet, import/export permits, and sometimes a microchip certificate. Our team will guide you through the specific requirements for your destination country.

How does the pet travel process work?
We handle everything from pre-travel vet checks and crate training to flight bookings and customs clearance. Your pet’s comfort and safety are our top priorities, and we provide regular updates throughout the journey.

What medical precautions should I take for my pet?
Ensure your pet is up-to-date on all vaccinations and has a recent health check. Some countries require specific tests or treatments before entry. We’ll inform you of all medical requirements and can recommend trusted veterinarians if needed.
